To request deletion of your account and associated data:

  1. Email beaworldchampion.app@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account.
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your account email address.

What we delete

  • Your account information (email, username, profile)
  • Predictions, league memberships, and leaderboard data tied to your account

What we may retain

We may retain the following information for up to 12 months unless a longer period is required by law:

  • Purchase records for tax/legal compliance
  • Aggregated or anonymous analytics data

Third-party services

Third-party services including Firebase, AdMob, and RevenueCat may retain data according to their own privacy policies.

Deletion timeline

We will confirm deletion of your account and associated data within 30 days.
