Delete Your Account — Be A World Champion

You can delete your account and associated data in two ways.

Option 1 — In the app (recommended)

  1. Open Be A World Champion
  2. Go to Account
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm deletion

Timeline: Your account and associated data are deleted immediately after you confirm. You will be signed out right away.

Option 2 — By email

If you cannot access the app (for example, you uninstalled it or cannot sign in), email us:

To: beaworldchampion.app@gmail.com

From: the email address linked to your account

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Include: your account email address

Timeline: We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

What we delete

When your account is deleted, we remove:

  • Your account information (email, username, profile)
  • Your predictions
  • Your league/tournament memberships
  • Your leaderboard data tied to your account
  • Your Firebase authentication account

If you own a private league/tournament, ownership may be transferred to another member. If no other members exist, the league/tournament may be deleted.

What we may retain

We may keep the following for up to 12 months, or longer if required by law:

  • Purchase records (for tax and legal compliance)
  • Aggregated or anonymized analytics data

Third-party services

Third-party services such as Firebase, Google AdMob, and RevenueCat may retain some data according to their own privacy policies.

Deleting your account in our app does not automatically delete all data held by these providers.
